Online Dr Appointment Booking System BCA Major

March 2025 - May 2025
Team of 5: Pritam Pandit, Tanisha Chakraborty, Roumik Das, Deep Choudhury, Manab Chanda

Purpose: The goal was to simplify and digitize the healthcare appointment process for better accessibility.

The platform was developed with a focus on simplicity, security, and scalability, making it ideal for clinics, small hospitals, or solo practitioners aiming to go digital.
This web-based application allows patients to:

  • Search for doctors using filters like location, specialization, availability....
  • View detailed profiles of doctors before booking.
  • Book appointments instantly with confirmation via email.
  • Manage their bookings, reschedule or cancel easily.

Doctors, on the other hand, can:

  • Set their availability schedule.
  • Accept or reject appointments.
  • Manage patient records and track appointments.
