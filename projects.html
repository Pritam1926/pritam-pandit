Institute Management System BCA Minor

Sept 2024 - Nov 2024
Team of 2: Pritam Pandit, Tanisha Chakraborty

Purpose: o streamline and digitize academic and administrative workflows of an institute by providing role-based portals for all users, ensuring better communication, transparency, and real-time access to essential data.

The Institute Management System is a full-featured web-based solution designed to digitize and simplify day-to-day operations of educational institutions. This platform provides personalized portals for students, faculty, and administrators, enabling seamless access to course details, class schedules, learning materials, exam updates, attendance records, and results. The system also includes an admission module where new students can explore available courses and apply directly through the portal. With role-based access control and centralized data management, the platform ensures security, efficiency, and enhanced user experience for all stakeholders.

  • Managing database schemas for user roles, attendance, and results.
